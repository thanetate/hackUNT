### HackUNT 2024 - Halloween Challenge

The task our team was presented with, was to design a Halloween themed app targeted for a supernatural audience. 

'Monster Match' is a halloween twist on traditional dating apps. This Tinder-like app connects ghouls, goblins, and other monsters with potential matches. Swipe through profiles of spooky characters and see if sparks fly.
